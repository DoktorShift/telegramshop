Shipping Costs How shipping is calculated:

For orders with physical items, shipping = flat rate + (total weight x per-kg rate).

If the order subtotal exceeds the free threshold, shipping is waived entirely.

Set all to 0 for free shipping on everything. Product weights come from the Inventory extension.

Returns & Refunds Let customers request returns through the bot. You review each request in the dashboard.
What is store credit?

When you approve a return, you can choose to refund the customer as store credit instead of sending sats back via Lightning.

The credit is saved to their account and automatically applied on their next purchase, reducing the invoice amount.

Why use it? You keep the sats in your wallet and the customer stays as a buyer. If disabled, you can only refund via Lightning (the customer must provide an invoice).
