# Terms and Conditions

## Telegram Shopping Extension

### Usage Terms

1. **Payment Processing**: This extension facilitates payment processing but does not hold funds. All payments are processed through LNbits wallets via Lightning Network.

2. **Telegram Bot**: The extension requires a Telegram Bot Token created via @BotFather. The merchant is responsible for their bot and must comply with Telegram's Terms of Service.

3. **Data Storage**: Customer information (Telegram chat IDs, order details, shipping addresses) is stored locally in the LNbits database. No data is shared with third parties.

4. **Inventory Integration**: Products are sourced from the LNbits Inventory extension. Stock levels are managed automatically upon successful payment.

5. **Shipping**: Shipping costs are configured by the merchant. The extension calculates costs but does not handle physical fulfillment. Merchants are responsible for shipping their products.

6. **Returns and Refunds**: Refund policies are determined by the merchant. The extension supports store credit refunds and Lightning refunds (coordinated via the messaging system). Partial refunds are available for restocking fees.

7. **Order Management**: Merchants are responsible for fulfilling orders and updating order status. The extension provides tracking tools but does not guarantee delivery.
